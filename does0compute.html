Does[0]compute? (pronounced “Does naught compute?”), is intended to foster a discussion about best practices in computational biology. Visit the DoesNaughtCompute Github for presentation slides, code, and sample data sets. Please bring a computer to use during the discussion. For more information please sign up for the Google group, Biocomputing Discussion Group.


Starting September 20th, the discussion group meets in 213 Borlaug Hall every Wednesday from 10:30 AM - 11:30 AM. We will be alternating weeks for the beginner and advanced sessions.
