Does[0]compute? (pronounced “Does naught compute?”), is intended to foster a discussion about best practices in computational biology. Visit the DoesNaughtCompute Github for presentation slides, code, and sample data sets. Please bring a computer to use during the discussion. Everyone is welcome to attend! For more information please sign up for the Google group, Biocomputing Discussion Group.


During spring semester 2019, the discussion group meets in 213 Borlaug Hall every other Friday from 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM. Please see schedule below for dates we will meet and topics we will cover. All programming levels are welcome!
